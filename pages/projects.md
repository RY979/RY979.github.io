### [Merchandising Dashboard (Power BI + SQL)](/projects/merchandising-dashboard/)
This dashboard explores sales performance, customer value, product category trends, and order behaviors to uncover actionable merchandising insights.        
Key metrics include CLV, repurchase rate, average order value, and monthly sales seasonality.

### [Customer Segmentation (Python)](/assets/projects/Sephora_project%20(1).html)
This project applies K-means clustering on key engagement and pricing metrics to segment Sephora products into distinct groups. It also uses regression analysis to examine how factors like love counts, reviews, price, and promotional flags influence product ratings and customer engagement.
